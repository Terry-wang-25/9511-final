Bonds and bond refunds

Follow the numbered steps in order, then choose whether to submit a claim or contact support.

← Back to renting overview

When to use this path: You moved out or are about to and need your bond returned or dispute a deduction.

  1. Confirm bond authority record

    Check online bond scheme for lodged amount and parties.

  2. Complete exit condition process

    Final inspection request, keys handover date.

  3. Keep copies of rent ledger

    Show no arrears or agreed payment plan.

  4. Challenge unfair deductions

    Itemise each deduction against evidence (fair wear and tear).

  5. Use formal bond dispute steps

    Follow your bond authority’s evidence and mediation process.
