Subscriptions and auto-renewal

Follow the numbered steps in order, then choose whether to submit a claim or contact support.

← Back to contracts overview

When to use this path: Free trial converted, renewal was unclear, or cancellation is blocked.

  1. Screenshot signup page

    What it said about price after trial and renewal date.

  2. Cancel through every channel

    App settings, web account, and email notice—keep confirmations.

  3. Request refund of wrongful charge

    List charge dates and why disclosure was inadequate.

  4. Dispute with payment provider

    If ignored, ask bank/card scheme about chargeback eligibility.

  5. Block future debits

    If needed, revoke direct debit authority after documented cancellation.
