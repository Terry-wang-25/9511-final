Reading Ruler

Reading ruler shows a highlight bar that follows your cursor, helping you focus on the current line. This feature is especially useful for users with reading or visual difficulties.

Instructions: while browsing, right-click your mouse or press Esc to quickly exit the reading ruler.

When enabled, moving your mouse over content shows a follow highlight bar.
