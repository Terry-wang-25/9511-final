Submit a claim

Topic: Digital goods, apps and downloads

This claim form is for paid digital products—software, apps, games, subscriptions or downloads—that are defective, not as described, or keep charging after cancellation. The fields below are tailored to this topic so we can triage your file correctly.

Before you start — have this ready

  • What you bought or booked, and the date of purchase or service
  • How the item or service failed (quality, description, fitness for purpose)
  • Repairs, refunds or responses already offered by the business
  • Platform (e.g. App Store), app name, version, subscription ID and error messages
Platform, product name, version, subscription or order ID, screenshots of errors or locked content.
